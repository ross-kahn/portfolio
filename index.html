Committee Tracking

Project Management
2013

One of my job responsibilities as Vice President of my Fraternity chapter was to "oversee the committees." Previously there was no formalized way to analyze the performance of the 17 committees, so I created and implemented a new process using Google Forms and some Google Code scripts. Using a scoring system based on the completion of weekly goals set by the committee heads, I was able to tactfully intervene on committees that showed a pattern of poor performance without bothering committees that were doing well.
